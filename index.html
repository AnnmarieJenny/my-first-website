Housing Relief Programs

Are you eligible for financial aid?

New York City and State officials have been working hard to make housing relief available to the most in-need New Yorkers. However, many still remain unsure of whether or not their eligible for benefits.

Answer a few questions below to determine your eligibility.

(1) Do you currently pay 30% or more of your household income on rent or your mortgage?



(2) Have you or others in your household been directly and adversely impacted by COVID-19?



(3) Do you have tangible evidence that your or someone in your household has been directly and adversely impacted by COVID-19? [This can includes a hospital bill, layoff letter from employer stating COVID-19 as the reason, etc]
